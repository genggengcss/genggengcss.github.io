About Me

➤ From July 2023, I am an Assistant Professor (特聘副教授) in School of Computer Science at Hangzhou Dianzi University, work in Knowledge Graph and Brain-inspired Intelligence Group.

➤ Sep. 2018 ~ June 2023, Ph.D student in Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Huajun Chen.

➤ Sep. 2014 ~ June 2018, B.S. student in Hohai University, advised by Dr. Ning Sun.

Research Interests

Knowledge Graph and Ontology, Zero/Few-shot Learning, Graph-based Reasoning, Neuro-Symbolic AI, XAI and related applications.


In my Ph.D life, I focus on Knowledge-driven Zero-shot Learning, with the help of Dr. Jiaoyan Chen from University of Oxford and The University of Manchester, Prof. Jeff Z. Pan from The University of Edinburgh, and Dr. Wen Zhang from Zhejiang University.
