About Me

➤ From Sep. 2018, I am a Ph.D student in Knowledge Engine Group at Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Huajun Chen.

➤ In 2014 and 2018, I got my B.S. degree at Hohai University, advised by Dr. Ning Sun.

Research Interest

Knowledge Graph (and Ontology), Zero/Few-shot Learning, Graph-based Reasoning, Neuro-Symbolic AI, XAI and related applications.


In my Ph.D life, I focus on Knowledge-driven Zero-shot Learning, with the help of Dr. Jiaoyan Chen from University of Oxford, Prof. Jeff Z. Pan from The University of Edinburgh, and Dr. Wen Zhang from Zhejiang University.
